##Deployment (Create-Stack)
 aws cloudformation create-stack --stack-name projectHA --region us-east-1 --template-body file://project.yml --parameters file://project-parameters.json

##Deployment (Update-Stack)
aws cloudformation update-stack --stack-name projectHA --region us-east-1 --template-body file://project.yml --parameters file://project-parameters.json

##Cleanup (Delete-Stack)
aws cloudformation delete-stack --stack-name projectHA --region us-east-1
